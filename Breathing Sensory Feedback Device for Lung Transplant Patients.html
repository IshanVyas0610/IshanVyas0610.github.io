Breathing Sensory Feedback Device for Lung Transplant Patients

Project information

  • Category: Digital Spirometer, Mechatronics, Bio-medical Device
  • Project date: April 2022
  • Project URL: github.com/IshanVyas0610/Gamified-Spirometer
  • Publication IEEE Xplore: Design and Development of a Low-Cost Spirometer with Interactive Gamification

  • A digital spirometer is developed along with a gamified application to engage patients during their breathing exercises and improve their lung strength.
  • The spirometer connects wirelessly with the app via Bluetooth, and an illustration is shown on the mobile screen as per the patient's lung output. A 2D Rocket Launcher test game has been developed for the initial prototyping phase of this product to engage users.
  • The game features a rigid body that moves between two points on the vertical axis, and the direction of movement is dependent on the inputs received from the sensor. For this prototype, the serial port was used to communicate between the ESP and Unity at a baud rate of 115200
  • A spirometry system was designed and developed consisting of a physical prototype and gamified application, achieving successful implementation of gamification to improve patient motivation during breathing exercises.
