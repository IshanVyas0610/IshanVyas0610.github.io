Human Detection System Using ESP32 Camera

Project information

  • Category: Electrooculography Controlled Motorized Wheelchair
  • Project date: September 2019
  • Project URL: www.example.com

  • Directed a team of three in designing a custom wheelchair for high spinal cord injury victims using CAD softwares like CATIA V5 and SOLIDWORKS.
  • Achieved exceptional safety margins by limiting maximum stress to 21.15% of the material's yield strength, ensuring durability and structural integrity for demanding conditions calculated using SOLIDWORKS.
  • Constructed a data acquisition system utilizing Arduino and ECG module for measuring and refining the electrical potential generated by the retina and cornea of the eye by 40%.
