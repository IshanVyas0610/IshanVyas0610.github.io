The Mimicking Hand

Project information

  • Category: Animatronics, Mechatronics, Robotics
  • Project date: April 2023
  • Project URL: github.com/IshanVyas0610/The-Mimicking-Hand

  • Modelled and manufactured a Mimicking Hand, seamlessly merging robotics with artistic principles to design innovative prosthetics. Leveraging my expertise in Blender 3.4 and SOLIDWORKS, I crafted a remarkable creation that showcases the perfect blend of form and function. Using advanced fabrication techniques such as 3D PLA printing with MakerBot Replicator+ and Creality Ender 3 Pro, as well as precise laser cutting with Epilog Helix and Fusion.
  • Guided a dynamic team of three members in spearheading the development of the actuator system architecture, culminating in an impressive 20% enhancement in accuracy, effectively replicating human-like movements with servo motors (Arduino).
  • Employed a meticulous approach of iterative testing to conquer design and manufacturing challenges, ultimately paving the way for substantial improvements in performance and functionality.
